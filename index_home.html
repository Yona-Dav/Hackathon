HANGMAN GAME - The rules

Hangman is a simple word guessing game

The Goal: Guess the word before your man gets hung!

How to play? The computer thinks of a word and the player tries to guess what is one letter at a time. The computer draws a number of dash equivalent to the number of letters in the word. If a player press or enter a letters that occurs in the word, the computer fills in the blanks with that letter in the right places. If the word does not contain the entered/pressed letter, the computer draws one element of a hangman's gallows and the letter "desapears". As the game progresses, a segment of the gallows and of a victim is added for every pressed/entered letter not in the word.

The levels:

  • Easy : The words are simple and the number of incorrect guesses before the game ends is 10.
  • Medium : The words are difficult to guess and the number of incorrect guesses before the game ends is 6.
  • Hard : The words are difficult to guess, the number of incorrect guesses before the game ends is 6 and you have 1:30 to guess the word.
